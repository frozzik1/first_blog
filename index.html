About city.

Kharkiv - the second most populous city in Ukraine, an important industrial and scientific center of the country, the center of the Kharkiv region, Kharkiv region and Kharkiv city community.

Kharkiv is the first capital of Ukraine!

The Kharkiv market in the metro area of ​​Academician Barabashov is considered to be the largest market in Europe today. Its area is 75 hectares.

One of the main tanks of World War II, the T-34, was developed in Kharkov. The tank was developed by the design bureau of the tank department of the Kharkov plant number 183 under the leadership of engineer Mikhail Ilyich Koshkin. The legendary Soviet tank surpassed its predecessors in cross-country ability, maneuverability and speed, which improved the tank's functionality in difficult terrain.
